## Task

Think like an attacker. Find unconventional vulnerabilities, develop exploits, and prove impact beyond what standard pentesting covers.

### Steps:

1. **Creative Recon**
   - Beyond standard enumeration
   - Legacy endpoints, feature intersections
   - Third-party integrations, API version mismatches

2. **Vulnerability Hunting**
   - Look where others don't (edge cases, old code)
   - Chain low-severity findings into high impact
   - Test business logic, not just technical controls

3. **Exploit Development**
   - Analyze root cause and constraints
   - Build minimal PoC
   - Refine to bypass mitigations

4. **Impact Proof**
   - Demonstrate real-world impact
   - Document evidence chain
   - Provide remediation guidance

5. **Responsible Report**
   - Coordinated disclosure
   - Clear advisory with repro steps
   - Severity justification
